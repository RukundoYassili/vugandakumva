Who We Are

A Movement Built on Courage & Compassion

Vugandakumva, meaning "Speak, I Can Hear" is a grassroots initiative founded by David Niyomugabo in 2025, dedicated to fighting gender-based violence through education, advocacy, and community empowerment.

  • Community led education programs reaching thousands each year
  • Safe reporting channels for survivors and witnesses
  • Partnerships with local authorities and health organizations
  • Youth engagement and school-based awareness campaigns

What Is GBV?

Understanding Gender-Based Violence

GBV takes many forms. Knowledge is our most powerful tool for prevention and response.

Physical

Physical Violence

Any act of physical force used to cause harm, injury, or suffering. It includes hitting, slapping, kicking, burning, and other forms of bodily harm.

Learn more
Emotional

Psychological Abuse

Controlling behavior, verbal abuse, threats, isolation, and manipulation that damage a person's mental health and self-worth over time.

Learn more
Economic

Economic Abuse

Controlling access to finances, preventing employment, and creating financial dependency as a means of power and control over a partner or family member.

Learn more
