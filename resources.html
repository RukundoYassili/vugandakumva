Step by Step

How to Report GBV

Reporting can feel overwhelming. Here's a simple, clear guide on what to do and who to contact.

1

Get to Safety

Remove yourself or the person at risk from immediate danger. Call 112 if life is threatened.

2

Seek Medical Care

Visit the nearest hospital or Isange One Stop Centre. Medical care is free for GBV survivors.

3

Report to Police

File a report at any police station. You have the right to a female officer if preferred. Keep copies of everything.

4

Get Ongoing Support

Connect with counseling, legal aid, and support groups to help you heal and rebuild your life.
